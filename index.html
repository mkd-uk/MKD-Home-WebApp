Welcome to My Personal Web App

This is a starter template for your personal web application. It includes:

  • A fixed header with app title
  • A hamburger menu that opens a sidebar
  • Right-side navigation links
  • A scrollable content area
  • A fixed bottom bar for scrolling to top
  • A clean, modern design with coordinated color theme

You can customize this template to suit your needs!
